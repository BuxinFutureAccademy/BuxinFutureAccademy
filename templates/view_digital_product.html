Step 1: Download
  1. Click the "Download" button to get your digital product
  2. Save the file to a location you'll remember
  3. Wait for the download to complete

  2. Select "Extract All" or "Extract Here"

  4. Wait for extraction to complete

  4. Start using your digital product!
