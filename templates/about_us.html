About BuXin

Pioneering the future of technology in Africa through innovative robotics, AI solutions, and sustainable electric vehicles.

Who We Are

BuXin is an innovative African technology company specializing in robotics, artificial intelligence (AI), and electric vehicles (EVs). Founded by a passionate team of engineers and tech enthusiasts, BuXin's goal is to leverage smart technology to solve real-world challenges faced by communities across Africa and beyond.

Our Mission

To empower Africa's youth and communities through cutting-edge robotics and AI solutions, sustainable electric vehicles, and inclusive STEM education — driving innovation, environmental stewardship, and economic growth.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in robotics and green technology by building intelligent products that improve lives, create jobs, and foster a sustainable future — with Africa at the forefront of technological advancement.

Core Values

Innovation

Pushing boundaries with creative, practical tech solutions.

Education

Investing in youth training and mentorship to cultivate future leaders.

Sustainability

Developing eco-friendly technologies that reduce carbon footprints.

Community

Building technology that solves real local problems and uplifts people.

Integrity

Maintaining transparency, quality, and ethical business practices.

What We Do

Robotics & AI Solutions

  • Designing AI-powered healthcare robots to assist hospitals in patient care and record management
  • Building mobile robots for various applications including offices, education, and industry
  • Developing intelligent automation systems to boost efficiency and reduce human error

Electric Vehicles

  • Creating self-driving electric scooters, mini tractors, minibuses, and electric buses tailored for African roads
  • Pioneering smart, green transportation solutions to lower pollution and improve mobility

Education & Training

  • Launching BuXin Future Academy, a STEM-focused school offering courses in robotics, AI, machine learning, and programming
  • Organizing robotics bootcamps, workshops, and competitions to inspire and nurture young talent
  • Providing free or subsidized Arduino kits, learning resources, and certificates to students

Major Achievements

Successfully built and deployed electric scooters and mini tractors

Developed AI assistant robots currently in testing phases for healthcare use

Won national robotics competitions, empowering youth teams and building local tech capacity

Launched online robotics and coding classes reaching hundreds of students

Future Plans

  • Scale up production of self-driving electric buses for public transportation
  • Expand BuXin Future Academy with a physical campus and online global reach
  • Develop a fully autonomous electric car tailored for African cities
  • Partner with governments, NGOs, and private sector to boost STEM education and green tech adoption
  • Grow BuXin into a global tech hub and innovation leader

Why BuXin Matters

Africa has immense talent and potential but lacks local technology infrastructure and education in emerging fields like AI and robotics. BuXin is bridging this gap by building homegrown technology solutions while empowering youth with practical skills. This not only drives economic development but also inspires a culture of innovation and self-reliance.
